Nazad na pocetnu

Pizza recipe

Description

Make dough: Pour warm water into a large bowl; dissolve yeast and sugar in warm water. Let stand until yeast softens and begins to form a creamy foam, about 5 to 10 minutes.

Mix in flour, 1 tablespoon olive oil, and salt until dough pulls away from the sides of the bowl.

    Ingredients

  • Meat
  • Onion
  • olive oil
  • Cheese
  • garlic

    Make the dough.

  1. Make the garlic oil, then brush it onto the grill grates.
  2. Cook the noodles
  3. Shape the dough and place it on the hot grill.
  4. Flip the crust and brush with oil.
  5. Top the pizza however you like
